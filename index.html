What to Wear

Dress Code

Semi-formal / cocktail attire

  • Men: a suit or a smart jacket with a collared shirt — tie optional.
  • Women: a cocktail dress or an elegant outfit.
  • Please avoid all-white looks (reserved for the bride) and very casual wear such as jeans, trainers or shorts.

The weather that week

Early April in Seoul is mild but cool — around 12–17 °C by day, with chilly evenings near 5 °C. It's cherry-blossom season and sometimes breezy, so bring a light coat or wrap.
